Why do many employers use a credit report?

A credit report is one of the best ways to get an idea of how an applicant handles their financial responsibilities. This is especially important for jobs in which an employee will be handing cash, sensitive financial information, funds or assets.

A credit report from ShareAble for Hires helps you evaluate how financially responsible your applicant is and whether there are any warning signs in their history that should give you pause.

ShareAble for Hires credit reports are delivered by TransUnion, a trusted consumer reporting agency that maintains 230 million consumer credit files domestically. Data is regulated by the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) and other applicable laws so you have more confidence the information you receive is reliable.
