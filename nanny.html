Nanny Background Check Services

Nanny background checks can help parents hire a trustworthy caregiver for their children.

Here’s what you get with ShareAble® for Hires nanny background check services:

National Criminal Report

Run a criminal history check and receive accurate results from both state and national databases, including the National Sex Offender Public Registry and the FBI’s Most Wanted. Learn more about our Criminal Reports

Detailed Credit Report

Benefits of Conducting a Nanny Background Screening

Broad coverage

When your child’s safety and well-being is on the line, you would want accurate data. ShareAble for Hires uses sophisticated record matching logic to match a criminal record to an offender. This means our babysitter background checks offer more accurate results and fewer false positive matches.

You’ll be able to sign up fast online, and once that sign-up is completed, you get access to screening tools right away. Compliant data
